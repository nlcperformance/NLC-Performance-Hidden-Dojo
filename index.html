The Ethos

NLC Performance is a private coaching practice for contact athletes (Rugby, BJJ, MMA) and high-stress professionals, not a mass-market program. I offer high-quality, bespoke plans.

Adaptation is driven by recovery, not just training. You and I track the 23 hours outside the gym using the Everfit app — focusing on CO2 tolerance, grip strength readiness, and sleep hygiene — before any physical training.

I cap my roster at 5 athletes. The goal is to equip you with the knowledge to manage your own physical load, not to keep you on a perpetual subscription.
