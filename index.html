The Ethos

NLC Performance is not a mass-market fitness program. It operates as a private coaching practice for contact athletes (Rugby, BJJ, MMA) and high-stress professionals.

I am detail-oriented, and if I spread myself too thin, the product you will get will diminish. High quality and bespoke plans only.

Most athletes train like professionals but treat recovery as an afterthought. Recovery causes Adaptation! You cannot out-train a fried central nervous system. We track the 23 hours outside the gym using the Everfit app—prioritizing CO2 tolerance, grip strength readiness, and sleep hygiene before we ever lift a kettlebell.

I cap my roster at 5 athletes at a time. The goal is not to keep you on a subscription forever; the goal is to teach you how to manage your own physical load until you outgrow the dojo.
