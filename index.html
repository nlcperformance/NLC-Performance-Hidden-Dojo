The Ethos

NLC Performance operates as a private coaching practice for combat athletes (BJJ, MMA, Rugby) and high-stress professionals. You are here because you demand an elite standard from yourself, but you are hovering on the edge of burnout—or crawling back from it.

Most driven individuals train like professionals but treat recovery as an afterthought. Recovery causes Adaptation. You cannot out-train a fried central nervous system. We track the 23 hours outside the gym using the Everfit app—prioritizing CO2 tolerance, grip strength readiness, and sleep hygiene before we ever lift a kettlebell.

I cap my active roster at 5 athletes at a time. The goal is not to keep you on a subscription forever; the goal is to teach you how to manage your own physical load until you outgrow the dojo.
